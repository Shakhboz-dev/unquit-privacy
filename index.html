Unquit — Maxfiylik siyosati

Kuchga kirgan sana: 2026-yil 22-iyun

Ushbu maxfiylik siyosati “Unquit” mobil ilovasi (bundan keyin — “Ilova”) foydalanuvchilarning ma’lumotlariga qanday munosabatda bo‘lishini tushuntiradi. Ilova yordamida siz kunlik vazifalar, eslatmalar, qaydlar, maqsadlar va odatlarni boshqarasiz.

Qisqacha: Unquit hech qanday shaxsiy ma’lumotingizni to‘plamaydi, serverga yubormaydi va uchinchi shaxslarga bermaydi. Siz kiritgan barcha ma’lumotlar faqat sizning qurilmangizda saqlanadi.

Biz to‘plamaydigan ma’lumotlar

Ilova quyidagilarni so‘ramaydi va to‘plamaydi:

  • ism, familiya, telefon raqami yoki email manzil;
  • ro‘yxatdan o‘tish yoki tizimga kirish (akkaunt talab qilinmaydi);
  • joylashuv (GPS) ma’lumotlari;
  • kontaktlar, rasmlar yoki boshqa shaxsiy fayllar.

Qurilmada saqlanadigan ma’lumotlar

Siz Ilovaga kiritgan barcha ma’lumotlar — vazifalar, eslatmalar, qaydlar, maqsadlar, tashlamoqchi bo‘lgan odatlar, statistika va sozlamalar — faqat sizning qurilmangiz xotirasida saqlanadi. Bu ma’lumotlar bizning yoki uchinchi tomon serverlariga yuborilmaydi va bizning ularga kirish imkonimiz yo‘q.

Ilova ruxsatlari

Ilova quyidagi ruxsatlardan foydalanadi:

  • Bildirishnomalar — vazifa va eslatmalarni belgilangan vaqtda ko‘rsatish uchun;
  • Aniq vaqtli signal (alarm) — eslatmalar aynan to‘g‘ri vaqtda kelishi uchun;
  • Qurilma qayta yoqilganda ishga tushish — telefon o‘chib-yonganda rejalashtirilgan eslatmalar yo‘qolmasligi uchun;
  • Vibratsiya — bildirishnoma kelganda titrash uchun.

Bu ruxsatlarning hech biri shaxsiy ma’lumotlaringizni yig‘ish yoki uzatish uchun ishlatilmaydi. Ilova internet ruxsatini ham so‘ramaydi.

Internet va tashqi xizmatlar

Ilova to‘liq oflayn ishlaydi va internetdan o‘zi foydalanmaydi — barcha shriftlar va kontent ilova ichiga joylangan. Ilova hatto internet ruxsatini ham so‘ramaydi.

Yagona istisno: agar “Aloqa” bo‘limida email yoki Telegram havolasini o‘zingiz bossangiz, tegishli tashqi ilova (pochta yoki Telegram) ochiladi. O‘sha ilovalardagi maxfiylik ularning o‘z siyosatlariga bo‘ysunadi.

Ilova reklama ko‘rsatmaydi va hech qanday tahlil/kuzatuv (analytics) vositalaridan foydalanmaydi.

Ma’lumotlarni uchinchi shaxslar bilan baham ko‘rish

Biz hech qanday ma’lumotni sotmaymiz, ijaraga bermaymiz va uchinchi shaxslar bilan baham ko‘rmaymiz — chunki biz umuman ma’lumot to‘plamaymiz.

Bolalar maxfiyligi

Ilova bolalardan ataylab hech qanday shaxsiy ma’lumot to‘plamaydi. Ilova umumiy auditoriya uchun mo‘ljallangan.

Ma’lumotlarni o‘chirish

Barcha ma’lumotlar qurilmangizda saqlangani uchun, Ilovani o‘chirib tashlaganingizda (uninstall) unga tegishli barcha ma’lumotlar ham qurilmadan butunlay o‘chiriladi. Bizda hech qanday nusxa qolmaydi. Shuningdek, ilova ichidagi sozlamalar orqali ma’lumotlarni qo‘lda tozalashingiz mumkin.

Xavfsizlik

Ma’lumotlaringiz qurilmangizdan tashqariga chiqmagani uchun, ularning xavfsizligi asosan qurilmangizning o‘z himoyasiga (parol, barmoq izi va h.k.) bog‘liq.

Siyosatdagi o‘zgarishlar

Ushbu maxfiylik siyosati vaqti-vaqti bilan yangilanishi mumkin. O‘zgarish bo‘lganda, yangilangan matn shu sahifada yangi “Kuchga kirgan sana” bilan e’lon qilinadi.

Unquit — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the “Unquit” mobile application (the “App”) handles user information. Unquit helps you manage daily tasks, reminders, notes, goals, and habits.

In short: Unquit does not collect any personal data, does not send data to any server, and does not share data with third parties. All information you enter stays only on your device.

Information we do NOT collect

The App does not request or collect:

  • name, phone number, or email address;
  • account registration or login (no account is required);
  • location (GPS) data;
  • contacts, photos, or other personal files.

Data stored on your device

All information you enter into the App — tasks, reminders, notes, goals, habits to quit, statistics, and settings — is stored solely in your device's local storage. This data is never transmitted to us or to any third-party server, and we have no access to it.

App permissions

The App uses the following permissions:

  • Notifications — to show tasks and reminders at the scheduled time;
  • Exact alarms & reminders — so reminders arrive at precisely the right time;
  • Run on boot — so scheduled reminders are not lost when the device restarts;
  • Vibration — to vibrate when a notification arrives.

None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit your personal data. The App does not even request the internet permission.

Internet and third-party services

The App works fully offline and does not use the internet itself — all fonts and content are bundled inside the App. The App does not even request the internet permission.

The only exception: if you yourself tap an email or Telegram link in the “Contact” section, the relevant external app (your mail app or Telegram) opens. Privacy within those apps is governed by their own policies.

The App shows no ads and uses no analytics or tracking tools.

Sharing data with third parties

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties — because we do not collect any data in the first place.

Children's privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. It is intended for a general audience.

Data deletion

Because all data is stored on your device, uninstalling the App permanently removes all of its data from your device. We keep no copy. You can also clear data manually through the App's settings.

Security

Since your data never leaves your device, its security depends mainly on your device's own protections (passcode, fingerprint, etc.).

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. When changes occur, the updated text will be published on this page with a new “Effective date.”
