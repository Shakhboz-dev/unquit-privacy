Ma'lumotlarni o'chirish — Unquit

Kuchga kirgan sana: 2026-yil 24-iyun

Ushbu sahifa "Unquit" mobil ilovasiga (developer: Unquit.app) tegishli ma'lumotlaringizni qanday o'chirishni yoki o'chirishni so'rashni tushuntiradi.

Qisqacha

Ma'lumotlaringizning deyarli barchasi faqat qurilmangizda saqlanadi — ilovani o'chirsangiz (uninstall), ular ham butunlay o'chadi. Bizda faqat siz o'zingiz yuborgan xabar yoki xatolik hisoboti qolishi mumkin; ularni email orqali o'chirib berishni so'rashingiz mumkin.

Qanday ma'lumotlar mavjud

  • Qurilmadagi ma'lumotlar — vazifalar, eslatmalar, qaydlar, maqsadlar, odatlar, statistika va sozlamalar. Bular faqat qurilmangizda saqlanadi, bizga yuborilmaydi.
  • Bizga yuborilgan ma'lumotlar (faqat siz tanlaganingizda) — "Muammo haqida xabar berish" orqali yuborgan matningiz va siz rozilik bergan texnik xatolik (crash) hisoboti.

1) Qurilmadagi ma'lumotlarni o'chirish

  1. Ilovani oching → Sozlamalar bo'limidan ma'lumotlarni tozalashingiz mumkin; yoki
  2. Ilovani telefoningizdan o'chirib tashlang (uninstall).

Shu zahoti barcha ma'lumotlaringiz qurilmadan butunlay o'chiriladi. Bizda ulardan hech qanday nusxa qolmaydi.

2) Bizga yuborilgan ma'lumotlarni o'chirishni so'rash

Agar siz "Muammo haqida xabar berish" orqali xabar yuborgan yoki xatolik hisobotini yuborgan bo'lsangiz va uni o'chirishni istasangiz:

  1. Unquit.app@gmail.com manziliga email yuboring.
  2. Mavzu (subject): "Ma'lumotni o'chirish so'rovi" deb yozing.
  3. Xatda xabarni taxminan qachon yuborganingizni yoki mazmunini qisqacha yozing — shunda biz uni topa olamiz.
  4. Biz so'rovingizni qabul qilamiz va ma'lumotni 30 kun ichida o'chirib, sizga tasdiqlab javob beramiz.

Qaysi ma'lumot saqlanadi va qancha muddat

  • Qurilmadagi ma'lumotlar: bizda umuman saqlanmaydi; faqat qurilmangizda turadi va o'chirish/uninstall qilsangiz darhol yo'qoladi.
  • Siz yuborgan xabar / xatolik hisoboti: siz o'chirishni so'ramaguningizcha saqlanadi. So'rov kelgach, 30 kun ichida o'chiriladi. Bu ma'lumotlar reklama, tahlil (analytics) yoki boshqa maqsadlarda ishlatilmaydi.

Data Deletion — Unquit

Effective date: June 24, 2026

This page explains how to delete, or request deletion of, data related to the "Unquit" mobile application (developer: Unquit.app).

In short

Almost all of your data is stored only on your device — uninstalling the app removes it completely. We may only hold a message or error report that you yourself chose to send; you can request its deletion by email.

What data exists

  • On-device data — tasks, reminders, notes, goals, habits, statistics, and settings. This is stored only on your device and is not sent to us.
  • Data sent to us (only when you choose to) — a message you send via "Report a problem", and a technical error (crash) report you agree to send.

1) Deleting on-device data

  1. Open the app → you can clear data from Settings; or
  2. Uninstall the app from your phone.

All of your data is then permanently removed from the device. We keep no copy of it.

2) Requesting deletion of data sent to us

If you have sent a message via "Report a problem" or sent an error report and want it deleted:

  1. Send an email to Unquit.app@gmail.com.
  2. Subject: "Data deletion request".
  3. In the email, tell us roughly when you sent the message, or briefly describe it, so we can find it.
  4. We will process your request and delete the data within 30 days, then confirm by reply.

What is retained and for how long

  • On-device data: never stored by us; it stays only on your device and is removed instantly when you clear data or uninstall.
  • A message / error report you sent: retained until you request deletion. Upon request it is deleted within 30 days. This data is not used for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose.
